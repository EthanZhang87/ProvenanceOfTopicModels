Crush s Coaster is a spinning roller coaster at Walt Disney Studios Park in Disneyland Paris The attraction officially opened on June as part of an expansion project in Toon Studio an area within Walt Disney Studios Park formerly known as Animation Courtyard The ride is themed to Disney Pixar s Finding Nemo film and named after Crush a green sea turtle voiced by Andrew Stanton from the movie Most of the roller coaster is enclosed and features dark ride special effects br On August following Disneyland Paris reopening Walt Disney Studios Park announced that the attraction was set to become part
