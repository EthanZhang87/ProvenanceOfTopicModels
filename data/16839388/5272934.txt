Avalanche Software is an American video game developer and subsidiary of Warner Bros Games based in Salt Lake City Utah It was founded in October by four programmers formerly of Sculptured Software including John Blackburn who is chief executive officer The studio was acquired by the games arm of The Walt Disney Company in May and spent the next ten years developing Disney related titles including the toys to life game Disney Infinity In May due to a declining toys to life games market Disney decided to close the games arm including Avalanche Warner Bros Games acquired the studio and
