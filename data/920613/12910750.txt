Your Friend the Rat is a American animated short film by Pixar written and directed by Jim Capobianco The special takes on the form of an educational film and stars rats Remy and Emile the main protagonists of Ratatouille who argue for the reconciliation of humans and rats They use historical facts presented via various styles of animation in a style similar to the Adventures in Music shorts
