title: Pheasantry
id: 32495993
A pheasantry is a place or facility used for captive breeding and rearing pheasants peafowls and other related birds which may or may not be confined with enclosures such as aviaries The pheasants may be sold or displayed to public or used as game birds Pheasantries may also be used for conservation and research purposes br br br See also br Aviculture br Falconry br Poultry br Hatchery br br br
