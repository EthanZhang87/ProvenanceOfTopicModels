The Wroc aw Africarium Polish Afrykarium is the only themed oceanarium devoted solely to exhibiting the fauna of Africa Located in Wroc aw Poland the Africarium is part of the Wroc aw Zoo The idea behind the Afrykarium is to comprehensively present selected ecosystems from the continent of Africa The Africarium houses multiple species of fish the hippos African fur seals manatees and many others The zoo houses over thousand animals its breadth extends from housing insects such cockroaches to the large mammals like the elephants on an area of over hectares br br br
