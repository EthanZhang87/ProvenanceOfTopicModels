title: Flat-ten engine
id: 3998587
A flat ten engine also known as a horizontally opposed ten is a ten cylinder piston engine with five cylinders on each side of a central crankshaft br There are no known flat ten engines which reached production br In the early s Chevrolet built several prototype flat ten engines as part of an aborted program for family of Modular Engines to replace the Chevrolet Turbo Air flat six engine This development program investigated flat engines with between two and twelve cylinders with the flat ten version being known as P pancake engine Although the program was initially intended to develop an engine for the Chevrolet Corvair which is rear engined with rear wheel drive the flat ten version was fitted to an experimental Chevrolet Impala a front engined car which was converted to front wheel drive br br br
