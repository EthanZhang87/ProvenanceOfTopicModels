The automotive aftermarket is the secondary parts market of the automotive industry concerned with the manufacturing remanufacturing distribution retailing and installation of all vehicle parts chemicals equipment and accessories after the sale of the automobile by the original equipment manufacturer OEM to the consumer The parts accessories etc for sale may or may not be manufactured by the OEM br The aftermarket encompasses parts for replacement collision appearance and performance The aftermarket provides a wide variety of parts of varying qualities and prices for nearly all vehicle makes and models br Consumers have the option of repairing their vehicles themselves the do it yourself or DIY segment or can take the vehicle to a professional repair facility the do it for me or DIFM segment br The aftermarket helps keep vehicles on the road by providing consumers the choice of where they want their vehicles serviced maintained or customized br br br Size of the automotive
