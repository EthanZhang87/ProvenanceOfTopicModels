title: Fuel filter
id: 2770697
A fuel filter is a filter used to screen out foreign particles or liquids from the fuel Most internal combustion engines use a fuel filter in order to protect the components in the fuel system br br br Filters for foreign particles br Unfiltered fuel may contain several kinds of contamination for example paint chips and dirt that has entered the fuel tank while filling or rust caused by moisture in a steel tank If these substances are not removed before the fuel enters the system they will cause rapid wear and failure of the fuel pump and injectors br The filters are normally made into cartridges containing a filter paper Fuel filters need to be maintained or replaced at regular intervals br br br Filters for foreign liquids br Some diesel engines use a bowl like design to collect water in the bottom of the filter as diesel floats on top of water The water can then be drained off by opening a valve in the bottom of the bowl and letting it run out until only the fuel remains br br br See also br br Fuel pump br Fuel tank br List of auto parts br br br
