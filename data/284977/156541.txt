The A side and B side are the two sides of phonograph records and cassettes and the terms have often been printed on the labels of two sided music recordings The A side usually features a recording that its artist producer or record company intends to be the initial focus of promotional efforts and radio airplay with the aim of it becoming a hit record The B side or flip side is a secondary recording that typically receives less attention although some B sides have been as successful as or more so than their A sides br Use of this language has largely declined in the st century as the music industry has transitioned away from analog recordings towards digital formats without physical sides such as downloads and streaming Nevertheless some artists and labels continue to employ the terms A side and B side metaphorically to describe the type of content a particular release features with B side sometimes representing a bonus track or other material br br br History br Conventions
