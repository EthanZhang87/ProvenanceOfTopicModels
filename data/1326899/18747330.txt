ship The monument has a square base of sea green granite It is positioned so that the fisherman is looking out over Gloucester Harbor The fisherman in the sculpture was modeled after Capt Clayton Morrissey a prominent Gloucester fisherman once the captain of the Effie M Morrissey The stone was purposely sculpted with a rough finish to make the fisherman look rugged Craske posed the fisherman
