title: Apparent wind indicator
id: 27222655
An apparent wind indicator as found on sailboat is a small weather vane or arrow that gives an indication of the current apparent wind direction This indication allows the skipper to set their sails or their course for best performance according to the points of sail br The device is often referred to as a Windex which is a trademark owned by Davis Instruments br br br Examples of modern apparent wind indicators for dinghies and open keelboats br br br
