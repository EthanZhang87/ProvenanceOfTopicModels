State Route SR is a mile long km state highway in the northwestern part of the U S state of Georgia It connects the Armuchee and Peachtree Corners areas within portions of Floyd Bartow Cherokee Fulton Gwinnett counties br br br Route description br br SR begins at an intersection with US SR near Armuchee within Floyd County It travels east crosses over the Oostanaula River and meets SR New Calhoun Highway NE It crosses into Bartow County and enters Adairsville In town it has an intersection with US SR Joe Frank Harris Parkway and an interchange with Interstate I Larry McDonald Memorial Highway
