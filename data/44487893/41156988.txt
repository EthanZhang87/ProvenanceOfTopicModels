Doge usually DOHJ DOHG or DOHZH is an Internet meme that became popular in The meme consists of a picture of a Shiba Inu dog accompanied by multicolored text in Comic Sans font in the foreground The text representing a kind of internal monologue is deliberately written in a form of broken English The meme originally and most frequently uses an image of a Shiba Inu named Kabosu though versions with other Shiba Inus are also popular br The meme is based on a photograph of Kabosu which became popular in late being named as Know Your Meme s top meme of that year The Shiba Inu had a notable Internet memes of the s br br br Structure br br Doge uses two word phrases in which the first word is almost always one of five modifiers so such many much and very and the departure from correct
