Pac Man VR is a video game by Virtuality set in the Pac Man universe The game is set in a first person perspective The game did not change any gameplay mechanics of the original game except adding a multiplayer feature br br br Gameplay br The game released for two of Virtuality s arcade VR systems The SU series unit had the player stand in a ring set at the waistline The player could turn their head and a tracking system built into the glasses would detect it and turn Pac Man s head in the game The SD series unit had players sit down and play more like a traditional arcade game while wearing the headset An adaption for the SU systems was released later on making Pac Man VR the only non shooting game ever released for SU systems br The game offered multiplayer through four cabinets networking up to four PAC MAN characters together enabling them to see talk and compete with each in the same virtual maze while still trying to outwit the ghosts Playing on the game cabinet itself cost five dollars for five minutes br br br br br br External links br Mirror of Virtuality s Pac Man VR Web Site br PAC MAN Leaps Into VR Virtuality Brings PAC MAN into the Third Dimension with PAC MAN VR br Video introduction to the game br SU software page
