Freeway is an action video game written by David Crane for the Atari and published by Activision in br One or two players control chickens who can be made to run across a ten lane highway filled with traffic in an effort to get to the other side Every time a chicken gets across a point is earned for that player If hit by a motorist a chicken is forced back either slightly or pushed back to the bottom of the screen depending on what difficulty the switch is set to The winner is the player who has scored the most points in the time allotted br br br Development br David Crane got the idea from the SCES in Chicago where a man tried to cross the street br br br Patch br If a player scored or more points on either Road or Road and mailed a photograph of the television screen Activision would send the player a cloth Save The Chicken Foundation patch with the Activision logo and the stylized running chicken image from the game package br br br Reception br br Freeway received an Honorable Mention for Most Innovative Game in at the Third Annual Arkie Awards br br br See also br br List of Atari games br List of Activision games br Frogger another video game with an animal crossing a road concept br br br br br br External links br Freeway at Atari Mania
