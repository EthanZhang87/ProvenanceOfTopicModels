Black Sails is an American historical adventure television series set on New Providence Island It was written to be a prequel to Robert Louis Stevenson s novel Treasure Island while also including depictions of several real life historical figures and fictionalized versions of actual historical events The series was created by Jonathan E Steinberg and Robert Levine for Starz It debuted online for free on YouTube and other various streaming platform and video on demand services on January The debut on cable television followed a week later on January Steinberg is executive producer alongside Michael Bay Brad Fuller and Andrew Form while Michael Angeli Doris Egan and Levine are co executive producers The show depicts treasure seeking pirates in their attempt to establish a Republic of Pirates br On July Starz renewed the show for a ten episode second season which premiered on January The early renewal six months before the first season premiered was based on the positive fan reaction to the show at the San Diego Comic Con The series was renewed for a third season on October and a fourth season on July both before the respective previous seasons had premiered
