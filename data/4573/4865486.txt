Code Rush is a documentary following the lives of a group of Netscape engineers in Silicon Valley It covers Netscape s last year as an independent company from their announcement of the Mozilla open source project until their acquisition by AOL It particularly focuses on the last minute rush to make the Mozilla source code ready for release by the deadline of March and the impact on the engineers lives and families as they attempt to save the company from ruin br After Andy Baio uploaded the documentary to his personal website for the release of Mozilla Firefox in director David Winton requested it be taken down pending his decision about future distribution under a free content license It has since been released under the Creative Commons BY NC SA US license br br br Featured Netscape employees br Jim Barksdale CEO br Scott Collins br Tara Hernandez br Stuart Parmenter then a year old open source volunteer br Jim Roskind br Michael Toy co author of Rogue br Jamie Zawinski br Brendan Eich br br br br br br External links br Code Rush at IMDb br Project Code Rush The Beginnings of Netscape Mozilla Documentary on YouTube br Code Rush on Vimeo br Code Rush is available for free viewing and download at the Internet Archive br Annotated Code Rush at the Wayback Machine archived br Interview with David Winton Director of Code Rush Mozilla Documentary
