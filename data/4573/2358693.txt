Request Tracker commonly abbreviated to RT is an open source tool for organizations of all sizes to track and manage workflows customer requests and internal project tasks of all sorts With seamless email integration custom ticket lifecycles configurable automation and detailed permissions and roles Request Tracker began as ticket tracking software written in Perl used to coordinate tasks and manage requests among an online community of users br RT s first release in was written by Jesse Vincent who later formed Best Practical Solutions LLC to distribute develop and support the package RT
