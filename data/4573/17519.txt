Larry Arnold Wall born September is an American computer programmer linguist and author He is best known for creating the Perl programming language and the patch tool br br br Personal life br Wall grew up in Los Angeles and then Bremerton Washington before starting higher education at Seattle Pacific University in majoring in chemistry and music and later pre medicine with a hiatus of several years working in the university s computing center before graduating with a bachelor s degree in Natural and Artificial Languages br While in graduate school at the University of California Berkeley Wall and his wife were studying linguistics with the intention of finding an unwritten language perhaps in Africa and creating a writing system for it They would then use this new writing system to translate various texts into the language among them the Bible For health reasons these plans were cancelled and they remained in the United States where Wall instead joined the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory after he finished graduate school br Wall is an active member of the New Life Church of the Nazarene He also works with his local church for Bible Quizzing for the Nor Cal district br br br
