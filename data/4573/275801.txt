TWiki is a Perl based structured wiki application typically used to run a collaboration platform knowledge or document management system a knowledge base or team portal Users can create wiki pages platform In October the company TWiki net created by Thoeny assumed full control over the TWiki project while much of the developer community forked off to join the Foswiki project br br br Major
