Isaac Bickerstaff Esq was a pseudonym used by Jonathan Swift as part of a hoax to predict the death of then famous Almanac maker and astrologer John Partridge br All Fools Day April now known as April the infallible Church written in his issue of Merlinus Almanac Swift projected three letters and a eulogy as an elaborate plan to predict Partridge s infallible death on March the anniversary of the famous Black Monday eclipse widely seen as discrediting to astrology br The first of the three letters Predictions for the Year published in January predicts among other things the death of Partridge
