title: Andika (typeface)
id: 67545443
Andika from the verb root for to write in Swahili is a sans serif typeface developed by SIL International for the Latin Greek and Cyrillic scripts It is designed for literacy programs and beginning readers but also has support for IPA transcription and a large number of diacritics The font offers four family members roman bold italic and bold italic br Andika supports OpenType and AAT technologies for advanced rendering features It is licensed under the SIL Open Font License OFL and can be downloaded free of charge br Version of the font includes over glyphs including stylistic variants and ligatures Stylistic variants include primer style forms of letters such as a g and t and the digit The Andika New Basic variant has a reduced number of characters and a significantly smaller file size br br Variant forms of many characters can be chosen in the word processor For example for non primer style a and g append ss to the name of the font in the font selection window Features are appended with a colon and linked with an ampersand Alternatively customized versions of the fonts can be created with TypeTuner prior to download that have those forms preset Features that may be chosen include two storey a loop tail g open top barred i and l with a bottom curl variant forms of capital capital Q with a crossing tail primer style t y with a straight tail Vietnamese style diacritics Serbian style italics in Cyrillic staveless tone letters slashed zero and automatic fractions br br br br br br External links br Andika homepage br TypeTuner Web homepage
