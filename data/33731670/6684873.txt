Animal Crossing is a social simulation video game series developed and published by Nintendo The series was conceptualized and created by Katsuya Eguchi and Hisashi Nogami In Animal Crossing the player character is a human who lives in a village inhabited by various anthropomorphic animals and can do various activities like fishing insect catching and fossil hunting The series is notable for
