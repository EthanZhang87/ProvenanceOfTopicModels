The Canadian men s national under ice hockey team is part of a three stage Program of Excellence beginning with the Under regional teams and ending with the National Junior Team The primary objectives of the Under program are to identify evaluate and condition players to the rigors of international competition by giving first exposure to off shore officiating ice surfaces and travel br The Under squad traditionally competes in the month of August during the off season to allow players to further develop skills with their respective junior teams in the winter following a week long camp A player roster is chosen by scouts and coaches from Hockey Canada to represent Canada on the international stage br The first National Men s Under Team was created in with the development of the
