A fantasy fiction magazine or fantasy magazine is a magazine which publishes primarily fantasy fiction Not generally included in the category are magazines for children with stories about such characters as Santa Claus Also not included are adult magazines about sexual fantasy Many fantasy magazines in addition to fiction have other features such as art cartoons reviews or letters from readers Some fantasy magazines also publish science fiction and horror fiction so there is not always a clear distinction between a fantasy magazine and a science fiction magazine For example
