Boxen The Imaginary World of the Young C S Lewis is a collection of stories created by C S Lewis Jack and his brother W H Lewis Warnie as children It was edited by Walter Hooper and first published posthumously by Collins May br The world of Boxen was created when Jack s stories about Animal Land and Warnie s stories about India were brought together In Surprised by Joy Jack explains that the union of Animal Land and India took place sometime in the late eighteenth century their eighteenth century not ours br During a time when influenza was ravaging many families the Lewis brothers were forced to stay indoors and entertain themselves by reading They read whatever books they could find both those written for children and adults Influenced by Beatrix Potter s animals C S Lewis wrote about Animal Land complete with details about its economics politics government and history as well as illustrations of buildings and characters br The first American edition was published by Harcourt Brace Javanovich October republished as Boxen Childhood Chronicles Before Narnia br br br
