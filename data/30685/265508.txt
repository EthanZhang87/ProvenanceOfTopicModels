title: Shadowlands (play)
id: 265508
Shadowlands is a play by William Nicholson adapted from his television film of the same name directed by Norman Stone and produced by David M Thompson for BBC Wales It debuted at the Theatre Royal in Plymouth on October before premiering at the Queen s Theatre in London on October The play is about the relationship between Oxford don and author C S Lewis and the American writer Joy Gresham br br br Synopsis br The story follows Lewis as he meets an American fan Joy Gresham whom he befriends and eventually marries The story also focuses on his relationship with his brother Warren Lewis and deals with his personal struggle Lewis preaches that one should endure suffering with patience but finds that the simple answers he had preached no longer apply when Joy becomes afflicted with cancer and eventually dies br br br Historical casting br br br Production history br The play opened at the Theatre Royal in Plymouth on October before transferring to the Queen s Theatre in London on October running until September The stage version gave Joy Davidman only one son instead of two The production was directed by Elijah Moshinsky and starred Nigel Hawthorne as Lewis with Jane Lapotaire as Joy It won Best Play in the Evening Standard Awards for Lapotaire was nominated for the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actress for her stage performance br Hawthorne successfully took the role of Lewis to Broadway playing at the Brooks Atkinson Theatre from November to April and again directed by Moshinsky It was nominated for the Tony Award for Best Play Hawthorne co starred in New York with Michael Allinson as Warren Lewis and Jane Alexander as Joy who was now given her maiden name of Joy Davidman Hawthorne won a Tony award for Best Actor while Nicholson picked up a nomination for Best Play br The first major revival of the play starring Charles Dance as Lewis and Janie Dee as Joy premiered at Cambridge Arts Theatre on September before touring the UK The production directed by Michael Barker Carven transferred to the Wyndham s Theatre on October for an eleven week season before transferring to the Novello Theatre where it ran from December to February br A new production of Shadowlands at Chichester Festival Theatre opened on April The production which was directed by Rachel Kavanaugh starred Hugh Bonneville as Lewis Liz White as Joy Gresham and Andrew Havill as Warren
