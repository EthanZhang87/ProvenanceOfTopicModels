In popular fiction a thieves guild is a formal association of criminals who participate in theft related organized crime The trope has been explored in literature cinema comic books and gaming such as in the Fafhrd and the Gray Mouser story Thieves House by Fritz Leiber and the role playing game Dungeons Dragons Though these more modern works are fictitious the concept is inspired by real world
