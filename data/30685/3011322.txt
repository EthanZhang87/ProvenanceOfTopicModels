Weird West aka Weird Western is a term used for the hybrid genres of fantasy Western horror Western and science fiction Western The term originated with DC s Weird Western Tales in but the idea is older as the genres have been blended since the s possibly earlier in B movie Westerns comic books movie serials and pulp magazines Individually the hybrid genres combine elements of the Western genre
