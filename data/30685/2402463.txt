Fanspeak is the slang or jargon current in science fiction and fantasy fandom especially those terms in use among readers and writers of science fiction fanzines br Fanspeak is made up of acronyms blended words obscure in jokes puns coinages from science fiction novels or films and archaic or standard English words used in specific ways relevant or amusing to the science fiction community br br
