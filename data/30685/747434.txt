Edmund Pevensie is a fictional character in C S Lewis s The Chronicles of Narnia series He is a principal character in three of the seven books The Lion the Witch and the Wardrobe Prince Caspian and The Voyage of the Dawn Treader and a lesser character in two others The Horse and His Boy and The Last Battle br In The Lion the Witch and the Wardrobe Edmund betrays his siblings to the White Witch while under her influence but as the story goes on he accepts the error of his ways He is redeemed with the intervention of Aslan and joins the fight against the witch Fulfilling an ancient prophecy
