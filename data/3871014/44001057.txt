Multi primary color MPC display is a display that can reproduce a wider gamut of color than conventional displays In addition to the standard RGB Red Green and Blue color subpixels the technology utilizes additional colors such as yellow magenta and cyan and thus increases the range of displayable colors that the human eye can see br Sharp s Quattron is the brand name of an LCD color display technology that utilizes a yellow fourth color subpixel It is used in Sharp s Aquos LCD TV product line particularly in models with screens inches across and larger br br br br br br External links br Aquos LCD TV Sharp website br Sharp LC LE E Review Technical assessment of Quattron TV by David Mackenzie at HDTVtest br GENOA Color Technologies
