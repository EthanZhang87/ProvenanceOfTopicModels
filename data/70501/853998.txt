The Manitoba Moose are a professional ice hockey team based in Winnipeg Manitoba Canada and a member of the American Hockey League AHL The team plays its home games at Canada Life Centre the home arena of its parent club Winnipeg Jets of the National Hockey League NHL br The franchise was founded in as the Minnesota Moose then playing in the International Hockey League IHL The Moose played fifteen seasons five in the IHL and ten in the AHL during their first tenure in Winnipeg This was followed by four seasons in St John s Newfoundland and Labrador during which the team was known as the St John s IceCaps The team returned to Winnipeg prior to the season br br br History br br br International Hockey League br Following the departure of the original Winnipeg Jets franchise to Phoenix
