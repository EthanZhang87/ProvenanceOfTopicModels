The Winnipeg Free Press or WFP founded as the Manitoba Free Press is a daily excluding Sunday broadsheet newspaper in Winnipeg Manitoba Canada It provides coverage of local provincial national and international news as well as current events in sports business and entertainment and various consumer oriented features such as homes and automobiles appear on a weekly basis br The WFP was founded in only two years after Manitoba had joined Confederation and predated Winnipeg s own incorporation The Winnipeg Free Press has since become the oldest newspaper in Western Canada that is still active
