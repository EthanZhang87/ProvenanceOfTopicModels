GNU Health is a free libre health and hospital information system with strong focus on public health and social medicine Its functionality includes management of electronic health records and laboratory information management system br It is designed to be multi platform supporting Linux distributions and FreeBSD on the server side It uses PostgreSQL as its database engine It is written in
