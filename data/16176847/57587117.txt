iOS is the twelfth major release of the iOS mobile operating system developed by Apple Aesthetically similar to its predecessor iOS it focuses more on performance than on new features quality improvements and security updates Announced at the company s Worldwide Developers Conference on June iOS was released to the public on September It was succeeded for the iPhone and iPod Touch by iOS on September and for the iPad by iPadOS on September Security updates for iOS continued for four years after the releases of iOS and iPadOS for devices unable to run the newer versions The last update
