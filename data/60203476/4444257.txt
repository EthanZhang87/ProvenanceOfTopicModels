The A V Club is an online newspaper and entertainment website featuring reviews interviews and other articles that examine films music television books games and other elements of pop culture media The A V Club was created in as a supplement to its satirical parent publication The Onion While it was a part of The Onion s website launch The A V Club had minimal presence on the website at that point br A website redesign placed The A V Club in a more prominent position allowing its online identity to grow Unlike The Onion The A V Club is not satirical The publication s name is a reference
