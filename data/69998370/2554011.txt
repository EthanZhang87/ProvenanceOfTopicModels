The Official Charts Company OCC or Official Charts previously known as the Chart Information Network CIN and the Official UK Charts Company legally known as the Official UK Charts Company Limited is a British inter professional organisation that compiles various official record charts in the United Kingdom Ireland and France br In the United Kingdom its charts include ones for singles albums and films with the data compiled from a mixture of downloads purchases of physical media and streaming The OCC produces its charts by gathering and combining sales data from retailers through market researchers Kantar and claims to cover of the singles market and of the album market and aims to collect data from any retailer who sells more than chart items per week br The OCC is operated jointly by the British Phonographic Industry and the Entertainment Retailers Association ERA formerly the British Association of Record Dealers BARD and is incorporated as a private company limited by shares
