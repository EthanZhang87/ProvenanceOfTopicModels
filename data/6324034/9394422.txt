Extras is a young adult science fiction novel written by Scott Westerfeld The novel was published and released by Simon Schuster on October and is a companion book to the Uglies series However Extras differs from its predecessors in that its protagonist is fifteen year old Aya Fuse not Tally Youngblood Despite the fact that Youngblood is not the main character she still appears in the book s later inhabitant has their own feed and obsessively tracks their face rank hoping to gain fame and lose their status as an extra Aya Fuse tries to win fame as an kicker or journalist filming stories with
